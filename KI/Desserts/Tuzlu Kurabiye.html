Tuzlu Kurabiye

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Cooking Steps

1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
2. Cream together the butter and sugars until smooth.
3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add to the batter along with salt.
5. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
6. Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheets.
7. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until edges are nicely browned.
